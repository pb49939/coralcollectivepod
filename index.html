Battling Marine Algae in 2020

November 2nd, 2020




Have you ever kept a reef aquarium that was an absolute algae magnet? Where no matter how low you kept your nitrate, how you kept your phosphate, algae was just an inevitable part of the hobby? Well, in this episode, we're going to fix it. We talk about leveraging heterotrophic bacteria (mainly a product called Vibrant) to apply a modern approach to eradicating unwanted algae in your reef.
